Join the all brother team of Ken and Joey Stagnaro. Between us, we have over 30 years of experience fishing the Monterey Bay, and we are dedicated to making your fishing adventure a fun and memorable one!


The Sea Stag III is a 43 ft twin engine sport fisher, designed to get you to the fish with speed and comfort. She cruises at a swift 17 knots, and features a heated cabin for your convenience.



Sea Stag III can be booked for private group charters, as well as on an individual basis. She is Coast Guard inspected and certified for 35 passengers.
