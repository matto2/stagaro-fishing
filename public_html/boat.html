Stagnaro Charter boats proudly presents the charter boat Velocity. Built by Yank Marine, Inc., in Tuckahoe, New Jersey, she was completed in California in 2005. Velocity is 60' long and is certified by the US Coast Guard to carry 66 passengers. (32-35 fishing). She features a full service galley and serves both breakfast and lunch items as well as your favorite beverage. Velocity is one of the only charter boats in Northern California equipped with passenger bunks and comfortable high-back seating. Her spacious walk-around deck and exterior seating make her a great choice for fishing, whale watching and scenic tours. Her experienced crew is dedicated to making your ocean adventure fun and memorable.

*** Velocity is a licensed beer and wine vessel. No outside alcohol may be brought on board. Coolers longer than 20" in length are not allowed.

Legacy is our newest addition in 2016. Built by Westport Shipyard in Washington State. She is 56 feet and US Coast Guard Certified to carry 46 passengers (30 fishing). Holding to tradition Legacy also features a Full Service Galley including Beer and Wine Service!
